Metric to Imperial Calculator for Carpenters

ChippyCal makes it easy to convert between metric and imperial measurements on site. Whether you're working from millimetres, centimetres, and metres or from feet and inches, you can switch instantly without breaking your workflow.

Built for real site work, this carpentry calculator helps you move between UK metric measurements and US imperial drawings quickly, without digging through menus or doing rough conversions in your head.

Why This Matters on Site

On real jobs, measurements are not always given in the system you want. You might be building from imperial drawings while buying materials in metric, or working with a team that mixes both. ChippyCal helps you convert quickly and keep moving.

Instead of stopping to work out millimetres to feet and inches by hand, you can convert instantly and carry on measuring, cutting, and setting out.

What You Can Convert

  • Millimetres to feet and inches
  • Centimetres to imperial measurements
  • Metres to feet
  • Feet and inches back to metric
  • Quick on-site conversions while working through cut lists and layouts

Built for Carpenters, Not Just General Maths

ChippyCal is designed as a practical construction calculator for carpenters and builders. It is built around site workflows, so conversions, geometry, run and rise calculations, and cut lists all sit together in one place.

That means fewer steps, less confusion, and faster answers when you are actually trying to get work done.
